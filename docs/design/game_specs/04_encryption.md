# Game Specification: "Encryption Protocol"

**Role:** The "Logic" / Sequences Trainer.
**Commercial Reference:** *The Room* / *Among Us* (Tasks).
**Hook:** "Unlock the Secrets."

## 1. Core Gameplay Loop
1.  **Interface:** A circular safe lock or a digital keypad.
2.  **The Puzzle:** A sequence of numbers is displayed on the dial: `3` -> `6` -> `12` -> `?`.
3.  **Action:**
    *   Dial: Rotate the wheel to the correct number (`24`).
    *   Keypad: Punch in the code.
4.  **Feedback:**
    *   **Correct:** Mechanical *Click*, Tumbler falls into place, Green light.
    *   **Incorrect:** *Clunk*, Red light, "Access Denied".

## 2. Dopamine Mechanics
*   **Tactile Satisfaction:** The rotation of the dial should have "ticks" (haptic feedback on mobile, click sounds).
*   **The Unlocking:** When the final tumbler hits, the door swings open to reveal...
    *   **Loot:** A pile of Bits or a lore fragment.
*   **Speed Cracking:** Solving 3 locks in under 10 seconds triggers "Master Thief" bonus.

## 3. Visual & Audio Targets
*   **Aesthetics:** High-tech security interfaces. Brushed metal, lasers.
*   **Audio:** Heavy mechanical sounds. Tumbers clicking. Hydraulic hisses.

## 4. Educational Tuning
*   **Concepts:** Linear sequences (n+2), Geometric sequences (n*2), Fibonacci, Prime numbers.
*   **Visual Logic:** Sometimes patterns are visual (Shapes with increasing sides).
